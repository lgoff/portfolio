About

Code. Design. Kale. Sunshine.

  • 1996

    Got dubbed the “creative child” of the family.

  • 2007

    Gained a reputation for breaking every electronic I touched and solemnly swore to never have a career dealing with technology.

  • 2012

    Circled the world on a boat and the bad luck with electronics continue as my ipad got thrown into a garbage compressor while out to dinner in Japan.

  • January 2013

    Hesitantly took a web design class to fill a science requirement. Discovered this gibberish code isn’t so scary after all. In fact, it’s kind of fun!

  • May 2013

    Graduate DePaul University.

  • January 13, 2014

    Enroll in the Web and Graphic Design program at Hennepin Technical College.

  • January 14, 2014

    Fall in love with design.

  • 2015

    Can't. Stop. Coding.

  • Current

    Will never again be able to not notice fonts, color schemes, and layouts on every street sign passed, restaurant menu looked at, and website visited.
